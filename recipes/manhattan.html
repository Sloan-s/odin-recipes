Manhattan

A classic whiskey cocktail made with only three ingredients. Snobs will always use rye, but bourbon tastes better.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rye whiskey or bourbon
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 to 3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 marashino cherry, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters to a chilled cocktail glass or shaker filled with ice. Stir until well chilled.
  2. Strain the mixture into your serving glass; ass one large ice cube and garnish with teh cherry
